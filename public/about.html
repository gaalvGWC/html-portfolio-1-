About Me

Born and raised in Miami, Florida. Gaby started Viola at 12 years old and attended the Florida International University under the tutelage of Michael Klotz.

Gabriella has attended the following summer music festivals: Interlochen Arts Camp, The Josef Gingold Chamber Music Festival, and the Heifetz International Institute of Music. She has also given a TED talk on the importance and understanding of classical music.

Aside from music, Gabriella also has a strong interest in technology and pursues coding.
